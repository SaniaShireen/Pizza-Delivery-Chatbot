Welcome to Pizzy's User Manual

  • If you want to know our best items, for example you can type "What are the best food items available" or "Suggest me your best items"
  • If you want to see the menu, type "menu"
  • While ordering, start your order with "I want...", so that chatbot will understand that you are placing your order
  • Specify the quantity you want before typing the item name. For example: I want 1 veggie pizza, 2 coke....
  • You can cancel your order by typing "I want to cancel my order"
  • Please follow the instructions given by the chatbot correctly
